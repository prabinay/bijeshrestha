About Me

Embarking on my educational journey, I commenced secondary schooling at Future Star Higher Secondary English Boarding School in Lamjung, followed by a specialized focus in Management at Prime College, where I graduated with a commendable CGPA of 2.36. Continuing my pursuit of knowledge, I obtained a Bachelor's degree in Business Administration from Atlantic International College, affiliated with Pokhara University, with a distinguished CGPA of 3.04. Diving into a comprehensive curriculum, my studies encompassed a myriad of subjects ranging from accounting and finance to marketing, international business, organizational behavior, and entrepreneurship, fostering a versatile skill set primed for future endeavors.

Transitioning into the professional realm, my tenure as a manager at Ecstasy Handmade saw me cultivate expertise across various domains, including meticulous financial oversight, astute production management, streamlined supply chain operations, proficient copywriting, strategic decision-making prowess, and meticulous brand maintenance.

Venturing into the realm of banking through an internship at RBB, I garnered firsthand experience in the intricacies of banking operations, with a focus on adept account management, stringent compliance adherence, and delivering unparalleled customer service. My subsequent stint at NIC Asia Bank further deepened my understanding of public banking practices, enriching my knowledge base.

Currently, in my role as a Trainee Assistant at Garima Bikas Bank's Finance Accounts And Treasury Department , I undertake the responsibility of overseeing branch expenditure, while concurrently immersing myself in the complexities of banking financial mechanisms, thus fostering a profound insight into the operational dynamics of the banking sector.
